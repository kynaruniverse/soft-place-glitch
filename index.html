Everything you need

Four ways to capture
any thought, instantly

Maké gives you four focused tools in one place — each designed for a different kind of thinking.

📝
Rich Notes
Full rich-text editing with bold, italic, headings, lists and colour. Live hashtag previews let you organise as you write.
⌨️
Code Snippets
Syntax highlighting for 16 languages. Real cursor, line numbers, one-tap copy. Your personal code library, always at hand.
🔗
Quick Links
Save any URL with a label and favicon. Tap to open. Your favourite sites as a beautiful home screen grid.
🗒️
Sticky Notes
Floating stickies you can drag, resize and colour. Perfect for quick reminders floating above everything else.
🌙
Ambient Sorting
Morning shows your notes. Afternoon your links. Evening your code. Maké adapts to how you think through the day.
🔍
Search Everything
Search across all content by type, tag, keyword or time. Star favourites and filter to just what matters right now.

Your data, your device

Nobody can see
your notes. Nobody.

Most apps store your notes on servers — which means they, and anyone who breaches them, can read your data. Maké works differently. Everything lives in a protected database on your own device. We have no server. We receive nothing.

🔒
Protected storage
When installed as an app, your data is marked persistent — clearing browser history leaves it completely untouched.
📴
No internet needed
Fully offline after the first load. No connection, no problem. Your notes are always there.
💾
Auto-backup file
Maké automatically writes a backup to a real file on your device. Restore everything in one tap if needed.
🚫
Zero tracking
No analytics, no cookies, no ads, no account. We genuinely have no idea you exist. That's by design.
